Some details about Lenovo Legion 5 15 - The Best Gaming Laptop under $1000

  • The Lenovo Legion 5 15 is our most recommended gaming laptop under $1000 right now because it is the most well-rounded machine for the price. It offers decent performing specs, excellent display, premium build quality, and features that are not readily available on most budget gaming laptops.


    It is powered by AMD's latest Ryzen 7 processor , which has 8 cores, and it can turbo boost up to 4.4 GHz. The CPU offers remarkable performance and efficiency, making it a better choice than its Intel counterpart. To handle gaming, the CPU is accompanied by a dedicated Nvidia GeForce RTX 3050 Ti GPU, which offers 4GB of graphics memory. The 3050 Ti is Nvidia's entry-level Ray Tracing GPU, but it can deliver pretty decent frame rates in most modern games at high settings with the help of DLSS. Apart from that, the Legion 5 15 also offers 16GB of RAM and 512GB of solid state drive for storage, which is adequate for most gamers; however, if you want more, both the RAM and storage drive (also the WiFi card) are upgradable.
